About

Where science meets
commercial strategy

I'm a pharmaceutical Product Manager with more than 13 years of experience across the GCC and Middle East, specializing in prescription medicines, OTC healthcare, ophthalmology, dermatology, and pediatric skincare. My work sits at the intersection of scientific product management, strategic marketing, and medical communication — translating clinical evidence into messaging that resonates with physicians, pharmacists, and patients alike.

Day to day, I partner with pharmaceutical principals, distributors, medical representatives, healthcare professionals, hospitals, and government procurement authorities to bring products to market and keep them growing. That means everything from brand strategy and launch planning to preparing clinical value dossiers for government tenders and training field teams on the science behind the sell.

I currently lead product management at Regional Pharma Trading DMCC in Dubai, representing European manufacturers across ten markets in the GCC and Levant. I hold a PMP® certification and postgraduate training in biotechnology — a foundation that keeps my commercial strategy grounded in real clinical substance.
